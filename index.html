I was born in Beijing, China. I spent the first four years of my life in China, mostly in Hubei. I visit my family in China every few years.
When I was four and a half I immigrated to the United States. I lived in Denver, Colorado for the next nine years, where I learned about multiplication and integrated into a new culture. I am still a huge fan of the Denver Broncos.
After middle school, I moved to San Diego and began high school in La Jolla. It was hard for me to leave Denver, but San Diego is a pretty cool place.
I am currently studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley. I can say for sure SoCal > NorCal.
